Fixed Deposit (FD)

At Apex Capital Trust, we offer secure and reliable Fixed Deposit (FD) solutions designed to help you grow your savings with guaranteed returns. Our FD options provide a safe investment avenue while ensuring capital protection and predictable earnings.

Whether you are looking for short-term or long-term investment plans, our expert advisors help you choose the right FD scheme with competitive interest rates, flexible tenure options, and hassle-free account management. Build your financial future with the stability and security of Fixed Deposits.

Enjoy the security of guaranteed returns with APEX Capital Trust Services' Fixed Deposit plans.

  • Safe investment option with assured returns.
  • Fixed interest rates throughout the tenure.
  • Capital protection with zero market risk.

Choose from a variety of FD tenures that suit your financial goals.

  • Short-term and long-term investment options.
  • Flexible maturity periods to match your needs.
