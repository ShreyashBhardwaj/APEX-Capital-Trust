Our Stratergy: The Three Bucket Theory

The Three Bucket Theory is a simple framework to manage wealth by dividing investments into three categories — Safety, Growth, and Advanced Strategies. Each bucket plays a unique role in balancing security, returns, and risk, ensuring a disciplined and diversified approach to financial planning. By following this strategy, customers can protect their capital, capture opportunities for long-term wealth creation, and still allocate a portion toward high-growth avenues. This balance not only safeguards their financial future but also provides the confidence and clarity needed to navigate market uncertainties while steadily growing their portfolio.

Bucket 1: Safety

  • ✔ Government Securities
  • ✔ Debt Instruments
  • ✔ Liquid Funds

Secure, low-risk, and highly liquid investments.

Bucket 2: Growth

  • ✔ Direct Stocks
  • ✔ Mutual Funds

Focused on long-term capital appreciation with moderate risk.

Bucket 3: Advanced

  • ✔ Options Strategies
  • ✔ Futures

Sophisticated strategies with higher risk and reward potential.
