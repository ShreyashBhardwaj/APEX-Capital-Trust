About Me

Nitin Kakkar, the founder of APEX Capital Trust, is dedicated to helping individuals grow their wealth with confidence. Our mission is to provide reliable, transparent, and result-oriented financial solutions tailored to each client’s unique goals and aspirations.

Under Nitin Kakkar’s leadership, we offer expertise in Retirement Planning, Goal-Based Planning, Tax Planning, and Financial Planning, backed by a wide range of investment products including Mutual Funds, SIFs, Life & Non-Life Insurance, PMS, Fixed Deposits, and our Stock Profiler. With APEX Capital Trust, your wealth is in trusted hands, and your financial future is our priority.

Our Mission

At APEX Capital Trust, our mission is to help individuals grow and protect their wealth with smart, actionable strategies. We make investing simple, focusing on long-term goals and providing clarity so clients can confidently navigate the financial landscape and achieve true financial independence.

We take a structured, stress-free approach to financial planning, combining expert insights with personalized guidance. Our goal is to empower clients to take control of their finances, make informed decisions, and build enduring wealth through sustainable and secure investment solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted partner for individuals seeking financial independence and lasting prosperity. We aim to simplify investing, providing the guidance, tools, and insights needed to make confident, informed decisions aligned with each client’s goals.

We envision a future where every investor can achieve sustainable wealth through clear strategies and personalized advice. By fostering strong relationships and sharing expert knowledge, we empower clients to take control of their financial journey and build a secure, prosperous tomorrow.
